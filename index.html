Why batches

One name, many vintages.

Aventus is not a single scent. Its formula has shifted across the years with ingredient rules and harvest to harvest variation, so collectors learn to read it by batch code. A 16M01 leans earthy and sweet on a bed of patchouli. A 15Y01 opens bright and pineapple forward. An 18K01 can last for days on a shirt.

We track these vintage batches, authenticate each one, and split them into decants. So instead of gambling on a full bottle, you can taste the difference between one year and the next, side by side, and find the batch that is yours.

Aventus Batch Reminder

Aventus batches naturally differ due to formulation variations per production run. Notes described here are based on testing under my skin chemistry and environment. Your individual experience may vary.

Oxidation and Maturation Notice

Some batches, especially F batches, develop deeper performance and smoother transitions after exposure to air. A fresh bottle may smell different after weeks of usage. Fragrances with lower skin performance may last significantly longer on fabric. For best performance, apply to both skin and clothing.

Testing Behavior

The same batch may present differently on:

  • skin vs clothing
  • freshly sprayed vs drydown
  • air conditioning vs outdoor heat
  • humid vs dry climate

Environmental Impact and Nose Fatigue

Philippine weather, sweat acidity, room temperature, and ventilation can affect projection and longevity. You may stop smelling the fragrance due to olfactory fatigue, even if others can still smell it around you.

No Return Due to Scent Subjectivity

Fragrance impressions are subjective. Returns or refunds are not accepted on the basis of personal scent preference or skin performance.

Decant Handling

Since these are transfers from original bottles to decants, minor variation can occur due to oxygen exposure during transfer and temporary agitation of scent molecules.

Fragrance Memory

Your past memory of how a batch smelled years ago may not be identical due to:

  • maturation
  • age of the juice
  • oxidation
  • storage conditions
  • your skin chemistry today vs before

Fair Expectations

Aventus is a fresh, airy, elegant fragrance, and not a beastmode projector. Even with its premium price range, it is not designed to fill an entire room or create a nuclear scent trail. Manage expectations accordingly. Aventus shines in sophistication, subtle presence, and refined personal aura rather than loud projection.
